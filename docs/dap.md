# DAP (Debug Adapter Protocol)

## Keybindings

- `sdp` - Toggle breakpoint
- `sdo` - Step over
- `sdu` - Step out
- `sdi` - Step into
- `sds` - Continue
- `sd-` - Terminate
- `sdt` - Open vsplit logs
- `sdm` - Toggle dapui

### Additional DAP Keybindings

- `sd<Tab>` - Dap Hover
- `sd<Space>` - Dap Scopes
